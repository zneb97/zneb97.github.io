Animatronic Independent Study

About

This is an ongoing study, with my progress and documentation here.

I took it upon myself as part of my penultimate semester to independently study and learn about animatronics through research, implementation, and design of animatronic control mechanisms. This takes the form of first implementing three very rough and accessible basic mechanisms found in animatronics: an eyeball, a hand, and a tentacle. These will serve as a basis from which I will learn from, iterate on, and prototype off of throughout the rest of the semester. These will all take place during 6, 2 week "sprints", culminating in one cohesive and consistent animatronic as a “final” project.
All of these efforts will be documented on this portfolio through CAD, photos, summaries and reflections.
